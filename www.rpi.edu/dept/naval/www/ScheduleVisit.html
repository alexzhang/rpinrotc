Schedule an Officer Interview

Prospective RPI NROTC candidates are highly encouraged to complete an officer inteview with our local unit if interested in joining our unit the coming Fall. The interview will allow us to get to know you better and ensure that RPI NROTC is a good fit for you. Additionally an interview conducted at the host unit will make for a much stronger application

Students are highly encouraged to visit to conduct the interview, but skype and phone interviews are also viable options.

Please call or email our Freshman Advisor, LT Chadwick Wilcox, to schedule an interview or visit.
