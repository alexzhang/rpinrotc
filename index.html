Captain's Welcome

Welcome to the RPI NROTC webpage. RPI NROTC is dedicated to train and develop Midshipmen mentally and physically for four years to ultimately lead Sailors and Marines in the fleet. We accomplish this through a mixture of classroom and hands on training in the fleet. We instill the Navy core values of Honor, Courage, and Commitment in everything we do.

We hope to provide all the information needed for perspective students and their families in order to assist in the application process as well as provide ample information of scholarships and the general education that RPI NROTC offers. Furthermore, we want to provide a smooth transition from high school life into life in the battalion. Our website is here as a resource to answer questions you may have as you go through the application process and provide you with the information you need prior to reporting to RPI.
